    Hi Nav Canada,

    My name is Ashley Tam and I am a web designer and developer living in Ottawa! I saw a post on the Ottawa SubReddit leading to your job posting and it looks like a position at Nav Canada would be a wonderful opportunity. My background is in web design but in the past few years I have turned my focus to web development and hope to develop my career further by gaining more experience in different programming languages.

    In my last project, I helped develop an mobile management application that tracked equipment and fleets for companies that provide energy, construction, and agriculture rental equipment and fluid transport. The application provided checklists and tracking abilities to both the workers and management which ensured the equipment was safe to use and all up to date. The current mobile application is now being used by several different fluid transport companies throughout Canada and the US. I believe my experience in working on this app allows me to understand the commitment to aviation safety and efficiency at Nav Canada.

    Although I do not have vast experience in Django, I am always ready for the challenge and motivated to learn new skills. To reintroduce myself to Python and Django I have created this interactive resume using the Django framework, HTML and CSS to help showcase my ability to learn quickly and apply the knowledge toward a workable application. This continued education has allowed me to stay ahead of the curve and deliver exceptional work to each employer I’ve worked for - both full-time and contract.
